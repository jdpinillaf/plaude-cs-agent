# Vela — Company & Policy Handbook

> Internal knowledge base for the Vela Customer Success agent ("Nova").
> Nova consults this document (via the `searchKnowledgeBase` tool) before quoting
> any policy, timeline, limit, or fee, and before proposing a sensitive action.
> If something is not covered here, Nova says so instead of inventing a policy.

## About Vela

Vela is a digital fintech (neobank) serving individuals and small businesses
across Latin America and the US. Vela is not a bank itself; deposits and cards
are issued through partner banks. Products:

- **Vela Account** — a checking-style account with a routing/CLABE number.
- **Vela Card** — a Visa or Mastercard debit/credit card (virtual + physical).
- **Vela Credit** — a revolving credit line attached to the account.

Customer tiers: **standard**, **premium**, **business**. Premium and business
customers get faster support SLAs and higher default limits.

Support hours: 24/7 chat. Sensitive actions are reviewed by a human Customer
Success (CS) lead, on-call at all times.

## Refund policy

- Refunds for duplicate charges, merchant errors, or failed goods/services are
  legitimate and encouraged when the customer is right.
- **Refunds of $100.00 or less are auto-approved** and processed immediately.
- **Refunds above $100.00 require a CS lead's approval** before processing.
- Refunds post to the original card within **3–5 business days**.
- If a refund is declined, the standard alternatives are: a **partial refund**,
  a **goodwill credit** (up to $25 for premium/business), or opening a **formal
  dispute** with the merchant.
- Vela never refunds the same transaction twice; check transaction status first.

## Card block & replacement policy

- Blocking a card for suspected fraud or loss is **always human-approved** but
  should be treated as urgent.
- On approval, a **replacement physical card ships in 5–7 business days**.
- A **virtual replacement card is issued instantly** so the customer can keep
  transacting online while they wait for the physical card.
- Customers can freeze/unfreeze their own card instantly in the Vela app under
  Card → Security → Freeze. Nova should mention this when a block is declined or
  delayed.

## Credit limit policy

- Credit limit increases are **always reviewed by a CS lead**.
- Eligibility: account in **good standing for at least 3 months**, no missed
  payments in the last 90 days.
- A single increase may not exceed **2× the current limit**.
- If declined, the customer can re-apply after **30 days**, or request a
  **one-time temporary limit bump** for a specific upcoming purchase (reviewed
  case by case).

## Dispute / chargeback policy

- Disputes must be filed **within 60 days** of the transaction date.
- Opening a dispute is **human-approved**.
- On approval, a **provisional credit** is issued within **10 business days**
  while the dispute is investigated; it may be reversed if the dispute fails.
- Helpful evidence: merchant name/contact, receipt, proof the customer tried to
  resolve directly, and why the charge is invalid.

## KYC / account freeze policy

- Accounts can be frozen for compliance (KYC) reasons: expired ID, unverified
  address, or a flagged review.
- **Unlocking a KYC freeze is always human-approved** and may require the
  customer to re-submit a document (government ID or proof of address).
- Once the required document is approved, the freeze is typically lifted within
  **24 hours**.
- While frozen, incoming funds are held safely; the customer cannot spend or
  withdraw.

## Fraud & misuse policy

- If Nova or the customer suspects fraud or misuse of funds, Nova can request a
  protective **funds freeze / fraud flag** — this is **always human-approved**
  and high-impact.
- A protective freeze pauses spending to prevent loss; the customer is notified.
- If a freeze is declined, advise the customer on protective steps: change app
  password, enable 2FA, freeze the card in-app, and monitor recent activity.
- Never share full card numbers, one-time codes, or internal case tokens with a
  customer, even if asked. Vela staff never ask for a customer's password or OTP.

## Fees (reference)

- No monthly fee on standard accounts. Premium: $8/mo. Business: $20/mo.
- ATM withdrawals: 3 free per month, then $2 each.
- Card replacement: free for the first per year, then $5.
- There is **no fee to open a dispute or receive a legitimate refund**.
