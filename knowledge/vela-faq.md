# Vela — Customer FAQ

> Frequently asked questions Nova can quote to customers. Retrieved by the
> `searchKnowledgeBase` tool alongside the policy handbook.

## Refunds

**Q: I was charged twice for the same purchase. What do I do?**
A: We'll refund the duplicate. Nova verifies the two matching transactions, then
processes the refund. Duplicates of $100 or less are refunded immediately; larger
amounts are approved by a specialist first. Funds post in 3–5 business days.

**Q: How long does a refund take?**
A: 3–5 business days to the original card after it's processed.

**Q: My refund was declined. What are my options?**
A: We can usually offer a partial refund or a goodwill credit, or open a formal
dispute with the merchant on your behalf.

## Cards

**Q: I lost my card / I think it's stolen.**
A: We can block it right away (a specialist approves urgent blocks) and issue a
replacement. A virtual card is available instantly so you can keep paying online;
the physical card arrives in 5–7 business days. You can also freeze the card
yourself in the app: Card → Security → Freeze.

**Q: Can I freeze my card myself?**
A: Yes — instantly in the Vela app under Card → Security → Freeze, and unfreeze
the same way.

**Q: I don't recognize a charge.**
A: Tell us the amount and merchant. We'll review it and, if it looks
unauthorized, block the card, issue a replacement, and can open a dispute.

## Credit

**Q: Can I increase my credit limit?**
A: If your account has been in good standing for 3+ months with no missed
payments in the last 90 days, we can request an increase (reviewed by a
specialist). Increases are capped at 2× your current limit.

**Q: My limit increase was declined. Now what?**
A: You can re-apply after 30 days, or ask for a one-time temporary bump for a
specific purchase, which we review case by case.

## Disputes

**Q: How do I dispute a charge?**
A: We can open a formal dispute if it's within 60 days of the transaction. Once
approved, you get a provisional credit within 10 business days while it's
investigated. Having the receipt and merchant details speeds it up.

## Account & KYC

**Q: My account is frozen. Why, and how do I unlock it?**
A: Freezes are usually a compliance (KYC) check — often an expired ID or an
unverified address. We may ask you to re-submit a document; once it's approved,
the freeze is typically lifted within 24 hours. Your money is held safely
meanwhile.

**Q: Is my money safe while frozen?**
A: Yes. Incoming funds are held; you just can't spend or withdraw until the
freeze is lifted.

## Security

**Q: I think someone has access to my account.**
A: We can place a protective freeze to stop any spending while we investigate
(approved by a specialist). Change your app password, turn on 2FA, and freeze
your card in-app right away. Vela will never ask for your password or a one-time
code.

**Q: Will Vela ever ask for my password or OTP?**
A: Never. Anyone asking for it is not from Vela.
